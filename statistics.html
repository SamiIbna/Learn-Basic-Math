Introduction to Statistics

Statistics is the science of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data. It helps make decisions based on data patterns.

Measures of Central Tendency

  • Mean: The average value. Example: Mean of 1, 2, 3 = (1+2+3)/3 = 2.
  • Median: The middle value in a sorted list.
  • Mode: The number that occurs most frequently.

Understanding Probability
