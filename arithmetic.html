Understanding Arithmetic

Arithmetic is the foundation of all mathematical concepts. It focuses on basic operations like addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

Addition

Addition is the process of combining two or more numbers to find their total or sum. For example:

Example: \( 5 + 3 = 8 \)

Subtraction

Subtraction involves finding the difference between two numbers by removing one from another. For example:

Example: \( 10 - 4 = 6 \)

Multiplication

Multiplication is repeated addition. For example, multiplying \( 3 \) by \( 4 \) means adding \( 3 \), \( 4 \) times:

Example: \( 3 \times 4 = 12 \)

Division

Division is the process of splitting a number into equal parts. For example:

Example: \( 12 \div 3 = 4 \)
