Understanding Geometry

Geometry is a branch of mathematics that deals with the properties and relations of points, lines, surfaces, and solids. It helps in understanding the physical world around us.

Basic Shapes

Learn about various shapes such as circles, triangles, rectangles, and polygons. Understand their properties and how to calculate areas and perimeters.

Angles

Explore the types of angles (acute, obtuse, right) and their properties. Learn how to measure angles and use them to solve geometry problems.

3D Shapes

Understand three-dimensional shapes like cubes, cylinders, and spheres. Learn to calculate their surface areas and volumes.

Real-Life Applications of Geometry

Geometry is used in:

  • Architectural design and construction.
  • Creating visual arts and designs.
  • Navigation and mapping.
  • Engineering and robotics.
